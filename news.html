TechHub -Ho West Second Edition Returns with a Big Bang!


Are you interested in learning all about computers? Are you interested in learning all about computers?

Are you a young person hungry to make a ground breaking change using IT?

Are you interested in developing a career in IT and Management?

Are you willing to sacrifice the Yuletide for a three-week intensive brain work?

Are you ready and willing to isolate and learn something new?

Are you between the ages of 14 and 45 and able to read and write?

If your answer to all the questions above is a YES, and you are a resident of the Ho West ... Constituency , then you are a prospective beneficiary.

As part of his programs in assisting the youth of the Ho West Constituency to build their capacities in the Fourth Industrial Revolution, the Member of Parliament for Ho West, Hon. Emmanuel Kwasi Bedzrah in collaboration with FixedByte an IT consulting firm from the UK announces an IT cum Management capacity building workshop for constituents. The youth in particular are to take advantage of this program and APPLY.

Various concepts in computer science including Artificial Intelligence, Software Development, Computer Hardware, Programming and app development, Telecoms and Network, Graphic Designs, Website Development, UI/UX Graphic Design, Video Editing and Videography among others will be taught intensively.

This life changing three weeks intensive learning experience starts on Monday January 2nd 2023 and ends on Monday, January 23rd, 2023.

Note: Fifty (50) prospective beneficiaries will be contacted for admission at the end of the selection process. All participants would be housed by the MP for the entire period of this training. You only need to bring along your own food to feed on.
